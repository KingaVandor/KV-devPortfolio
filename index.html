Profiles & contact

After 16 years as a landscape architect I am now pursuing a second career as a software developer.

I started learning code last winter, following a move from London to Scotland, where opportunities in my original profession are limited, particularly on a senior level.



The initial necessity to learn something new soon became a choice.



I realized that I find studying code a fulfilling and exciting task that leaves more room for enjoyment than my old job did for a while.
